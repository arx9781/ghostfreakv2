Spotify is a popular music streaming service that offers a wide range of music genres and artists. Integrating Spotify into your Next.js app can provide a seamless and enjoyable user experience, allowing users to easily access their favorite tracks and discover new music.

In this guide, we'll explore how to integrate Spotify into a Next.js app using the Spotify Web API. We'll cover topics such as authentication, fetching user data, and displaying playlists and tracks.
